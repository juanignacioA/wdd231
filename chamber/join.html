Non-Profit Membership

Cost: Free ($0/year)

Designed strictly for registered non-profit organizations and community groups.

Benefits:

  • Basic business directory listing.
  • Access to general chamber networking events.
  • Community project collaboration.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $50 / year

Ideal for small businesses and startups looking to build local connections.

Benefits:

  • Standard business directory listing.
  • 10% discount on Chamber training and workshops.
  • Acknowledge in the new member newsletter.
  • Access to member-only networking events.

Silver Membership

Cost: $150 / year

Perfect for growing organizations seeking higher visibility in Dolores.

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Benefits included.
  • Advanced directory listing with logo and social links.
  • 20% discount on Chamber events and training.
  • 1 Spotlight position on the home page sidebar per year.
  • Free access to business development seminars.

Gold Membership

Cost: $300 / year

Premium tier providing maximum exposure, elite advertising, and VIP access.

Benefits:

  • All Silver Benefits included.
  • Prominent directory listing featured at the top of searches.
  • 30% discount on all event tickets.
  • Permanent Spotlight position on the Chamber Homepage.
  • VIP invitations to exclusive board-level events.
  • Free booth/space during the annual Chamber Fair.
